## Key Insights

### Genre Distribution

Spotify playlists are dominated by a few major genres, particularly EDM, Pop, and Rap. This indicates a strong user preference for mainstream and high-energy music, while other genres have relatively lower representation.

---

### Music Release Trends

There is a noticeable increase in music releases after 2015, highlighting the impact of streaming platforms on content production. The growth suggests that digital distribution has made it easier for artists to release music at scale.

---

### Top Artists

A small group of artists contributes a significant portion of the tracks in the dataset. This reflects a concentration of content among high-output or highly featured artists.

---

### Overall Insight

Spotify’s ecosystem is driven by dominant genres, increasing content production, and a concentration of output among top artists, indicating a competitive and trend-focused music industry.
