# Spotify Music Trends Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Spotify music data to identify patterns in genre distribution, content growth over time, and artist contribution. The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that explain trends in the music streaming industry.

---

## Objective

To explore Spotify data and understand:

* Which genres dominate playlists
* How music releases have evolved over time
* Which artists contribute the most content

---

## Dataset

* Source: Spotify Songs Dataset
* Format: CSV
* Key fields used:

  * track_artist
  * track_name
  * playlist_genre
  * track_popularity
  * track_album_release_date
  * year (derived column)

---

## Tools and Technologies

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Jupyter Notebook (Data Cleaning and Visualization)

---

## Methodology

### Data Cleaning

* Removed missing values from key columns
* Removed duplicate records
* Converted release date to datetime format
* Extracted year for time-based analysis

### Data Analysis

* Aggregated genre data to identify dominant categories
* Analyzed yearly trends to study growth in music releases
* Identified top artists based on number of tracks

### Visualization

* Donut chart for genre distribution
* Line chart for music release trends
* Horizontal bar chart for top artists

---

## Key Insights

### Genre Distribution

Spotify playlists are dominated by EDM, Pop, and Rap, indicating strong user preference for mainstream and high-energy music.

### Music Release Trends

Music releases increased significantly after 2015, reflecting the growth of streaming platforms and digital distribution.

### Top Artists

A small group of artists contributes a large share of tracks, showing concentration of content among top creators.

### Overall Insight

Spotify content is driven by dominant genres, rapid growth in releases, and a few high-output artists.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis highlights how streaming platforms have influenced music production and consumption. The dominance of certain genres, increasing content volume, and concentration among top artists reflect a competitive and trend-driven industry.
